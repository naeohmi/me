About

Naomi has 10 years of experience - 6 years as a full time Software Engineer and 4 years as a High School Teacher. For the past 5 years she worked as a Software Development Engineer at Adobe on the Globalization, Core Services team where she led internationalization and localization for Adobe products. She speaks regularly at international software engineering conferences, contributes to open source, and volunteers teaching girls to code.

Naomi went to university in New Zealand, then worked as a teacher across Asia and West Africa. She is currently based in Seattle (where she grew up) and enjoys weekends outside in the mountains.

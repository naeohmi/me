About

With over six years of experience as a Software Development Engineer, I lead the internationalization and localization development work for global Adobe products, such as Adobe Fonts, Analytics, and the Content Authenticity Initiative. I work primarily with JavaScript, Python, and various DevOps technologies, ensuring the quality and accessibility of Adobe's software solutions across different languages and regions. I am also passionate about sharing my knowledge and expertise in the software engineering field, especially in JavaScript, Internationalization, Unicode, Emoji Encoding, and Data Visualization. I speak regularly at international conferences, contribute to open source projects, and volunteer teaching girls to code. I enjoy learning new skills and technologies, and applying them to solve real-world problems to create positive social impact.

About

Naomi brings over a decade of cross disciplinary experience in software engineering, cybersecurity, and education leadership.

She is currently a Software Engineer at T-Mobile, contributing to core engineering tools across the organization. During her five years as a Software Development Engineer at Adobe, she built high impact features, served on technical committees, and presented at international engineering conferences. She has earned her Master's in Cybersecurity and Leadership from the University of Washington, graduating with honors while simultaneously conducting ethical bug bounty research.

Naomi's technical depth is complemented by a global perspective shaped by her earlier years teaching in classrooms across Asia and serving with the Peace Corps in West Africa. She enjoys weekends outside in the mountains with her dog.
